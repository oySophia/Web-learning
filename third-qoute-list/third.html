June 2, 2005

My first day of the trip! I can't believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because i'm not on a Segway, I wasn't able to bring a whole lot with me: Cellphone, digital camera, and a protein bar. As Lao Tzu would have said, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.
